About Our Principal

Dr. Milind Madhav Khanapurkar is the Principal at the Cummins College Of Engineering For Women, Nagpur. He obtained his B.E in Electronics Engineering, M.Tech in Electronics Design Technology, MBA in Marketing & Management, and his Ph.D. degree in the area of Electronics Engineering. He has published 29 International Journals and 9 National Journals. He has membership in various statutory bodies namely the Board of Governance, Academic Council at CCOEW. He is also a member of the Professional Society namely IEEE, IETE, ISTE, CSI, etc. He has experience of more than 30 years. He has been awarded the Best Teacher award of GHRCE Nagpur.
